In this project of coffee sales analysis, you have to analysis
on the basis of the coffee shop and its revenue.

The key analytical point to cover in this project are:

--How does sales vary by day of the week and hour of the day?

--Are there any peak times for sales activity?

--What is the total sales revenue for each month?

--How do sales vary across different store locations

--What is the average price/Order per person

--Which products are the best- selling in terms of quantity and revenue

--How do sales vary by product category and type?
